## Interaction Contract

### "Run Ingest" Button

- Label: **"Run Ingest"**
- Placement: in the section divider row, right-aligned (same row as the divider label)
- Appearance (idle): `px-3 py-2 rounded-lg text-xs font-medium border bg-slate-800/60 text-slate-400 border-slate-700/50 hover:text-slate-200 transition-colors`
- Appearance (loading): disabled, label changes to **"IngestingтАж"**, add `opacity-60 cursor-not-allowed`
- Appearance (success): border switches to `border-emerald-500/30 text-emerald-400 bg-emerald-500/15` for 2 seconds, then reverts
- Appearance (error): border switches to `border-red-500/30 text-red-400 bg-red-500/15`, label **"Ingest failed тАФ click to retry"**, persists until next click
- Action: `POST /api/recall/ingest` тАФ triggers incremental JSONL ingestion
- After success: re-fetch `/api/recall/stats` to refresh panel stats

### Stat Display (read-only)

All 4 stats are read-only, fetched from `GET /api/recall/stats` on mount. No user interaction beyond "Run Ingest".

### Polling

No auto-refresh. Stats are fetched once on mount. "Run Ingest" is the manual refresh trigger.
